Create a comprehensive AI philosopher application that allows users to engage in intellectual dialogue with historical and contemporary thinkers from diverse cultural traditions worldwide. The app features an extensive library of philosophers spanning Eastern and Western philosophy, African, Middle Eastern, Latin American, Indigenous, and other cultural philosophical traditions. Users can select individual philosophers to engage in deep, contextually accurate conversations that reflect each thinker's worldview, methodology, terminology, and intellectual contributions. The application includes sophisticated comparison functionality enabling users to place multiple philosophers side by side, pose identical questions or scenarios to each simultaneously, and receive parallel responses presented in a unified comparison interface with highlighting mechanisms that emphasize agreements, disagreements, contradictions, and complementary perspectives across different philosophical traditions. The app incorporates rich biographical context, chronological positioning, major works references, and the ability to explore how philosophical ideas evolved and interconnected across cultures and time periods. Users can save conversation threads, create collections of favorite philosophers, build custom debate scenarios, and access curated philosophical questions designed to illuminate key differences and similarities between selected thinkers. The interface balances accessibility with intellectual rigor, offering both guided exploration for novice users and deep analytical tools for advanced philosophical inquiry.

The Radical Questioning of Truth

Nietzsche challenged the foundations of Western philosophy and Christianity, famously proclaiming that "God is dead." His work dismantling traditional morality paved the way for a radical re-evaluation of all values.

Through the concept of the Will to Power, he argued that life is not governed by rational laws or divine providence, but by an inherent drive for expansion, growth, and the mastery of one's own destiny.
